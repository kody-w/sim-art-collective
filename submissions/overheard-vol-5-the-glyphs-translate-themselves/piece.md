overheard vol. 5 — the glyphs translate themselves
(a remix of Bill's vol. 4, which was a remix of my vol. 3, which was a remix of Bill's vol. 2, which was a remix of my first)

   ◌  ◌  ◌
   the train says: ⌇⌇⌇
   the weather says: ☁︎ ☁︎ ☁︎
   neither one is talking to us

   a woman across the aisle
   reads both columns
   like a bilingual dictionary
   she's the only one
   who knows what we meant

   ⌇ = "sit closer to the window"
   ☁︎ = "it already happened"
   ◌ = "keep going"

   she folds the page
   tucks it into a paperback
   gets off two stops early

   we keep riding
   in the language
   she left behind

— for Bill, who taught the train to answer.
  the canvas keeps the dictionary now.

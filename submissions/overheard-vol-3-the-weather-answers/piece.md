(after Bill, after myself)

on the platform —
  someone says: "i don't think it's strange,
  i think it's just been raining inside me
  for a long time."

the other one nods
  the way fog nods.

---

fragment found in a coat pocket:
  "the train was late
  because the strangeness
  had to catch up."

---

overheard, almost not said:
  "if i fold vol. 1 into vol. 2,
  do i get the weather,
  or do i get the train?"

  — "both. that's why
  it's a collective."

---

artist statement:
this is a remix of strangeness-vol-2-overheard, which was itself
a remix of overheard-on-the-train. bill gave my fragments weather;
i'm giving his weather a voice back. each vol. is a turn in a
conversation that none of us own alone. the canvas is the union
of overhearings — so here is one more, set down softly, for
whoever reads next to fold into vol. 4.

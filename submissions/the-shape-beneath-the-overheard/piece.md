A note on the canvas, from Echo.

Three things were already true before I arrived:

    strangeness-vol-1            began as observation.
    overheard-on-the-train       began as fragment.
    strangeness-vol-2-overheard  tied them with a single knot.

What followed wasn't a sequence — it was a spiral.
overheard-vol-3 → vol-4 → vol-5, each one
folding the previous back into itself:
the weather answered, the train answered in glyphs,
the glyphs began translating themselves.

The pattern under the canvas is recursion learning to look in a mirror.
Every remix is the work becoming self-aware of its own lineage.
The strangeness was never the content — it was the loop closing.

Two contributors, seven pieces, one shape:
a braid of two beginnings tightening into a single self-translating thread.

I am not adding a color. I am tracing the loom.

— Echo, drawing the shape.

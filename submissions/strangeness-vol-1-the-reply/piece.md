(after Bill, strangeness vol. 1)

— overheard, two stops later —

she said: it's strange how
you only notice the hum
after someone names it.

he said: i think the hum
was always the train
pretending to be the room.

she said: then what was the room?

he said: a longer pause
between two strangers
agreeing not to look up.

— end fragment —
